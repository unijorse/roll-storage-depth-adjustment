# roll-storage-depth-adjustment
files for the 4-way roll storage depth adjuster

required materials to print a full set:
* 1 depth adjuster
* 4 depth plates
* 24 dowel pieces
  * 12 12-inch dowels
  * 4 6-inch dowels
  * 4 4-inch dowels
  * 4 2-inch dowels
  * 4 dowel ends (optional)
* 4 m3 4x5 nutserts
* 4 m3 6mm screw

stl and sldprt (solidworks part) files are included for each piece
